Products

Minifits products are all ethically sourced and made from the highest quality GOTS certified textiles.

Subscription Box

The Minifits quarterly subscription is box filled with 10 high quality baby garments made with Global organic Textile Standard cotton. Each quarter you recieve a bigger size as your baby grows at a low price of $99! Take the style quiz to choose your items!

Secondhand Subscription Box

Want to shop secondhand? It's one of the most eco-friendly practices you can adopt! Shop our secondhand subscription boxes for $49 a quarter and help reduce textile waste going into landfills and incinerators.

Buy-Back Program

All Minifits members are eligible to participate in our buy-back program. Send us your old Minifits clothing that your baby outgrows and recieve credit toward your next box! Then, we recycle the old clothing and help keep them out of landfills!

Our Mission

Minifits mission is to produce high quality clothing for your baby & the planet. All clothing are ethically made and sources, and are produced by Global Organic Textile Standards (GOTS). MiniFits mission is to reduce textile waste going into landfills (21 billion per year from the US alone!) and create a closed-loop system.

Sustainability is our middle name. All of MiniFits business practices center around staying conscious of our environmental impact. Many industries greatly impact the environment, and the retail industry is a huge contributer to waste, greenhouse gasses, and overall environmental degradation. Minifits is here to end the cycle.

Membership

All Minifits members subscribe to a quarterly subscription service and receive clothing specific to your baby's need. When registering, members take the style quiz to tailor their quarterly box to their needs.

Did you know your baby needs new clothes every few months? MiniFits makes this process simple and easy, all while helping you to live a more sustainable lifestyle! Sign up for a Minifits subscription and help out your baby and the planet (all while never having to leave your home!).
